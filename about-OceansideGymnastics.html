About Us

Mission / Values / Visions

Oceanside Gymnastics and it's dedicated staff are committed to improving young lives in fitness and building self confidence through the wonderful sport of gymnastics.

We offer programs from beginner to competitive team gymnastics for children walking to 18 years. By participating in a foundational sport like gymnastics, students will develop fundamental movement patterns and sport skills while gaining strength, flexibility, speed, and agility. Early participation in sports increases physical fitness while building social skills.

Through progressive skill training we provide a safe and positive learning environment that will encourage and inspire students. Students will set goals and master new skills while developing self-esteem, mental and physical strength, that is not only useful in gymnastics, but will also serve them well in their future.
