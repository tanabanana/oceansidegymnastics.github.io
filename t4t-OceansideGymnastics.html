Tricks 4 Treats Designated Meet Weekend

Our Halloween-themed meet is coming up and here's all you need to know! Our meet will include a Team March-In Costume Contest for athletes and a Coaches Costume Contest for coaches (please dress with discretion) so we encourage all participants, coaches, and judges to dress-up. Trick or treat style goodie bags will be given to all athletes.
