Tricks 4 Treats Invitational

Our Halloween-themed meet is coming up and here's all you need to know! Our meet will include a Team March-In Costume Contest so dressing up for athletes, coaches, AND judges is extremely encouraged! All athletes will recieve an All-Around award and a trick or treat style goodie bag, and there will be a 50% award placement.
