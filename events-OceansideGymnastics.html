Birthday Parties

Is your child's birthday approaching and you're not a fan of the planning, hosting, and clean-up involved in a birthday party? No problem, let Oceanside Gymnastics host the party for your child and guests. We do it all from the set up to keeping the kids active and having fun all the way down to the cutting and serving of the cake. The best part is when the party is over you don't have a mess to clean up! Let our staff do it all for you while you can sit back and relax! To schedule your child's birthday party or to learn more please come by the front desk or call 760-941-0202.
